# Changelog

All notable changes to the Aura English project will be documented in this file.

## [1.4.2] - 2026-03-25

### Added
- **11 New Vocabulary Decks**:
  - Modern Slang & Gen Z English (45 cards)
  - Culinary & Dining Out (45 cards)
  - Health & Wellness (45 cards)
  - Entertainment & Pop Culture (45 cards)
  - Nature & Environment (45 cards)
  - Phrasal Verbs Masterclass (45 cards)
  - Advanced Idioms (45 cards)
  - False Friends (Faux-amis) (45 cards)
  - Finance & Crypto (45 cards)
  - AI & Future Tech (45 cards)
  - Psychology & Human Behavior (45 cards)
- **Agent Rule**: Added automatic changelog update requirements for the AI assistant.

### Changed
- **Version Bump**: Updated application version to `v1.4.2`.
- **Catalogue Update**: Synchronized `cardCount` and metadata for all new decks in the API.
- **Notification Fix**: Switched to a 7-day revolving buffer of unique one-off notifications instead of a single repeating daily trigger.
- **Legal Documentation**: Added interactive "Privacy Policy" and "Terms of Use" pages to the website.
- **UI Fix**: Corrected the Progress Ring in Statistics module to support 1% granularity and smooth visuals.
